The Best Movies According to Leslie

My top 3 movies of all-time.

About Time

Brief Storyline: "About Time" is a romantic comedy-drama that follows Tim Lake, who discovers that the men in his family have the ability to travel through time. Using this power, Tim aims to improve his life and relationships, particularly with his love interest, Mary. However, he soon realizes that time travel cannot solve all of life's problems and must learn to appreciate the present moment.

Why People Love It: People love "About Time" for its heartwarming and thought-provoking storyline, which blends romance, humor, and poignant life lessons. The film's message about the importance of cherishing everyday moments resonates with many viewers, making it a beloved favorite.


Elemental

Brief Storyline: "Elemental" is an animated movie set in a world where four elements—fire, water, land, and air—live together. The story follows Ember, a fiery young woman, and Wade, a go-with-the-flow water guy, as they navigate their differences and discover how much they have in common.

Why People Love It: People love "Elemental" for its unique and imaginative world-building, stunning animation, and the heartwarming relationship between the main characters. The film's themes of acceptance, understanding, and finding common ground resonate with audiences of all ages.


Inside Out

Brief Storyline: "Inside Out" is an animated film that takes place inside the mind of a young girl named Riley, where her emotions—Joy, Sadness, Anger, Fear, and Disgust—struggle to guide her through a major life change. As Riley adjusts to a new city and school, her emotions must work together to help her navigate the ups and downs of growing up.

Why People Love It: "Inside Out" is loved for its innovative and insightful portrayal of emotions and mental health. The film's creative concept, emotional depth, and powerful message about the importance of all emotions make it a standout favorite among viewers.
